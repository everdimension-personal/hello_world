Morse encoder/decryptor







Enter the line in small letters or the morse code using spaces between letters (symbols).

If there is s mistake in your line, you'll see '@' sign in the output which will mean, that there are some unknown or false-input symbols in your input.

The " || " (double pipe) sign means the space between two words. When entering morse code by yourself put this sign between the words and don't forget spaces before and after pipes!

Please do not enter the morse code and english words at the same time - this won't get the right result!
